French Toast

Description

Delicious, tender, and fluffy cooked breakfast.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Directions

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl
  2. Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides. Transger to the hot skillet and Cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve hot.
