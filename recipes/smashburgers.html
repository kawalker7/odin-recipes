Smashburgers

Description

This is a smash burger. In order to get this, you take 2.5 ounce balls of meat and smash them.

Ingredients

For the copycat smash sauce

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons pickle juice
  • 1 tablespoon diced pickle

For the burger seasoning mix

  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt

For the toppings

  • 1/2 head green leaf lettuce, washed and dried
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 medium onion, sliced
  • 6 slices American cheese

For the smashburger

  • 1 pound 80/20 ground beef
  • 4 brioche hamburger buns
    • 2 tablespoons butter, divided

For the toppings

Method

  1. Make Your Secret Sauce:

    2. In a small bowl combine the mayonnaise, mustard, pickle juice, and diced pickles. Set aside until you're ready to assemble your burgers.

  2. Mix Together Burger Seasoning:

    3. In another small bowl combine the kosher salt, black pepper, and seasoned salt

  3. Prepare the burgers:

    4. Loosley divide the ground beef into 4 even balls. Season the balls of beef with 1/8 teaspoon burger seasoning mix. Don't worry about completely covering them. You can add more seasoning when they are cooking.

  4. Cook the smashburgers:

    5. Wrap a heavy skillet (cast iron works well) with some foil, you will use this to smash the burgers. I use a smaller 8-inch cast iron skillet, but any smaller size will work.

    In a large skillet or griddle set over medium-high heat and add one tablespoon of butter. Once the butter has melted, add the beef balls.

    Place the foil wrapped skillet or a spatula over the beef balls and smash them into flat disks. Smash each burger for 10 seconds, one at a time, applying a lot of pressure to it. Repeat until all the burgers are smashed.
