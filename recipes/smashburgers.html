Smashburgers

Description

This is a smash burger. In order to get this, you take 2.5 ounce balls of meat and smash them.

Ingredients

For the copycat smash sauce:

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons pickle juice
  • 1 tablespoon diced pickle

For the burger seasoning mix:

  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt

For the toppings:

  • 1/2 head green leaf lettuce, washed and dried
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 medium onion, sliced
  • 6 slices American cheese

For the smashburger:

  • 1 pound 80/20 ground beef
  • 4 brioche hamburger buns
    • 2 tablespoons butter, divided

Steps:

  1. Make Your Secret Sauce:

    2. In a small bowl combine the mayonnaise, mustard, pickle juice, and diced pickles. Set aside until you're ready to assemble your burgers.

  2. Mix Together Burger Seasoning:

    3. In another small bowl combine the kosher salt, black pepper, and seasoned salt

  3. Prepare the burgers:

    4. Loosley divide the ground beef into 4 even balls. Season the balls of beef with 1/8 teaspoon burger seasoning mix. Don't worry about completely covering them. You can add more seasoning when they are cooking.

  4. Cook the smashburgers:

    5. Wrap a heavy skillet (cast iron works well) with some foil, you will use this to smash the burgers. I use a smaller 8-inch cast iron skillet, but any smaller size will work.

    In a large skillet or griddle set over medium-high heat and add one tablespoon of butter. Once the butter has melted, add the beef balls.

    Place the foil wrapped skillet or a spatula over the beef balls and smash them into flat disks. Smash each burger for 10 seconds, one at a time, applying a lot of pressureto it. Repeat until all the burgers are smashed.

  5. Season burgers with more mix and continue to cook:

    6. Season the smashburgers with another 1/8 teaspoon burger seasoning mix. Allow them to continue to cook for about 90 seconds until the juices start to bubble from the burger. Use a sturdy metal spatula to flip the burgers, being sure to scap any caramelization.

  6. Add cheese to the burgers

    7. After you've flipped the burgers, add cheese and continue to cook for 2-3 minutes, until burgers are cooked to desired doneness. The burger is so thint hat there's no need to check temperature. It will be done if the burger has a good caramelization on the outside. You can continue to cook additional time depending on your preference.

  7. Toast the buns:

    8. While the burgers cook, toast the buns. In a large skillet over medium heat add the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter. Add the buns, cut side down and toast for 30 seconds, until golden. Repeat until all buns are toasted.

  8. Build and serve the smashburgers:

    9. Build smashburger by adding some sauce, approximately 1 tablespoon, to the bottom of the bun. Add lettuce over the top of the sauce, then add a smashburger, and finally top with sliced red onion and tomato. Continue until all smashburgers are assembled.

    Serve immediately with fries and a cold drink.
