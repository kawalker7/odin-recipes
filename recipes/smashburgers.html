Smashburgers

Description

This is a smash burger. In order to get this, you take 2.5 ounce balls of meat and smash them.

Ingredients

For the copycat smash sauce

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons pickle juice
  • 1 tablespoon diced pickle

For the burger seasoning mix

  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt

For the toppings

  • 1/2 head green leaf lettuce, washed and dried
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 medium onion, sliced
  • 6 slices American cheese

For the smashburger

  • 1 pound 80/20 ground beef
  • 4 brioche hamburger buns
    • 2 tablespoons butter, divided
